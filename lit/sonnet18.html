Word notes:

  • Shall: should.
  • Compare: liken, equate.
  • Thee: you (singular).
  • Summer's day: metaphor for youth, beauty, and warmth.

Paraphrase:

Explanation:

The speaker is contemplating whether or not to compare the person they are addressing to a summer's day, which is traditionally associated with beauty and warmth.

Historical/cultural context:

The speaker is expressing admiration for the person they are addressing, using the conventions of courtly love poetry popular in Shakespeare's time.
